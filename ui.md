# Website Description – Legal Services (Disability Law)

## 1. Website Purpose
This website represents a professional law firm specializing in **Disability Law and Social Security Benefits (SSDI / SSI)**.  
The main goal is to:
- Provide clear information about legal services
- Build trust and credibility
- Encourage visitors to request a **Free Evaluation**
- Convert visitors into qualified legal leads

The website should communicate:
- Professionalism
- Trustworthiness
- Compassion
- Legal authority

---

## 2. Target Audience
- Individuals seeking disability benefits (SSDI / SSI)
- People needing legal representation for appeals
- Families helping relatives with disability claims
- Adults aged 30–65
- Users looking for reliable and professional legal support

---

## 3. Brand Tone & Voice
- Professional
- Reassuring
- Clear and simple language
- Respectful and empathetic
- Authoritative but friendly
- Avoid slang or casual wording

---

## 4. Visual Style & Design System

### Color Scheme
Primary colors:
- **Navy Blue (#0B1F33)** – trust, law, authority
- **Gold (#C9A24D)** – success, quality, prestige

Secondary colors:
- White / Light Gray for background
- Soft neutral tones for readability

### Design Style
- Clean and modern legal website
- Corporate and professional appearance
- High contrast between text and background
- Elegant typography (serif or professional sans-serif)
- Subtle gold accents for buttons and highlights

### UI Principles
- Large hero section with main CTA
- Clear section separation
- Easy navigation
- Mobile responsive
- Accessibility-friendly
